Common Areas & Amenities

All residents and their guests are expected to treat common areas with care and respect. Amenities are provided for the enjoyment of all residents.

✅Residents may bring up to 2 guests into amenity spaces during operating hours.
✅The Party Room and rooftop BBQ areas may be booked by any resident in good standing.
✅All amenities are available daily within their posted operating hours.
❌Food and beverages are not permitted in the Fitness Centre.
❌Children under 14 must be accompanied by a resident adult in all amenity areas.
❌Personal belongings may not be stored or left in common areas or hallways.
❌Smoking and vaping are prohibited in all common areas and within 9 metres of any building entrance.
ℹ️All amenity bookings must be made at least 48 hours in advance via the Resident Portal.
ℹ️A $200 refundable damage deposit is required when booking the Party Room.
🔇

Noise & Quiet Hours

Residents are responsible for ensuring their unit does not produce excessive noise that disturbs neighbours. Quiet hours are strictly enforced.

✅Normal household sounds are permitted daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
✅Renovations and moves may be conducted Monday–Friday 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM and Saturday 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
❌Loud music, gatherings, or disruptive noise after 10:00 PM or before 8:00 AM on any day.
❌Drilling, hammering, or loud power tools before 8:00 AM weekdays or before 10:00 AM weekends.
❌Renovations of any kind on Sundays or Statutory Holidays.
ℹ️Noise complaints can be reported to the concierge or submitted through the Resident Portal at any time.
ℹ️Repeated violations may result in a formal notice from the Corporation and potential fines under the Condo Act.
🐾

Pet Policy

Pets are welcome at Yorkdale Condominium II, subject to the following rules. All pets must be registered with the Corporation before or at move-in.

✅Cats and dogs under 25 kg are permitted with prior registration with the Corporation.
✅Pets must be kept on a leash or in a carrier in all common areas at all times.
✅Pets must use the service elevator when traveling through common areas.
❌No more than 2 pets per unit.
❌Pets are not permitted in the pool area, Fitness Centre, or Party Room.
❌Owners must immediately clean up after their pets in all common areas and on Corporation property.
❌Pets causing persistent noise disturbances are subject to Corporation action.
ℹ️Register your pet by completing the Pet Registration Form available in the Resident Portal.
ℹ️A valid rabies vaccination certificate may be required at the time of registration.
🅿️

Parking

Parking spaces are assigned to units. Visitor parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis for guests with a pass obtained from the concierge.

✅Each unit owner is entitled to their designated parking space(s) as defined in their title documents.
✅Visitor parking is available on Level P1 — 2-hour limit without a pass from the concierge.
✅Visitor parking passes are available at the concierge desk — maximum 2 passes per unit per day.
❌No mechanical repairs, fluid changes, or car washing in the parking garage.
❌Bicycle storage areas are for bicycles only — no other items may be stored there.
❌No parking in fire lanes, loading zones, or unmarked spaces at any time.
❌Resident parking spaces may not be sub-let to non-residents without Corporation approval.
ℹ️Unauthorized vehicles in reserved spaces may be towed at the owner's expense without further notice.
ℹ️EV charging stations are available — book via the Resident Portal. Applicable fees apply.
📦

Move-In & Move-Out

All moves must be pre-arranged through the Resident Portal to ensure the service elevator is available and the lobby and common areas are protected.

✅Book your move date through the Resident Portal at least 72 hours in advance.
✅Moving is permitted Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM only.
✅The service elevator will be reserved exclusively for your use during your scheduled booking.
❌No moves on Sundays or Statutory Holidays.
❌Furniture, boxes, or packing materials may not be left in hallways or common areas at any time.
❌Passenger elevators must not be used for moving furniture or large items.
ℹ️A $500 refundable deposit is required before your move, returned after lobby and elevator inspection.
ℹ️Moving companies must be accompanied by the resident at all times while in the building.
ℹ️A Certificate of Insurance from your moving company may be required before access is granted.
♻️

Waste & Recycling

Proper waste disposal keeps our building clean and compliant with City of Toronto waste management requirements. Thank you for doing your part.

✅Garbage and recycling must be taken to the waste room on your floor.
✅Cardboard must be flattened before placing in recycling bins.
✅Large items and e-waste can be scheduled for bulk collection via the Resident Portal.
❌Hazardous materials (paints, solvents, batteries) must not be placed in regular waste bins — visit toronto.ca for disposal options.
❌Garbage bags or recycling must not be left in hallways, stairwells, or at elevators.
❌Large furniture or appliances must not be placed in the waste room without prior arrangement.
ℹ️Waste rooms are accessible 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM daily. Contact the concierge for bulk item scheduling.
ℹ️Recycling follows the City of Toronto Blue Bin program — visit toronto.ca for the full list of accepted materials.

The complete Declaration, By-laws, and Rules of Yorkdale Condominium II are available in the Resident Portal under the Documents section. In the event of any discrepancy between this guide and the registered legal documents, the registered documents prevail.
